uORover - University of Ottawa Mars Rover Team

The University of Ottawa Mars Rover Team is a student-led, multidisciplinary organization dedicated to the design and construction of a rover suitable for the exploration of extraterrestrial environments. Through hands-on experience, we aim to foster a love for STEM fields as well as inspire the next generation of engineers and scientists.

Our Competitions

uORover tries to participate in many competitions every year. our usual roster includes:

  1. CIRC - The Canadian International Rover Challenge takes place in Drumheller Alberta every summer in August. uORover has been lucky enough to participate three times, and we are currently working on our fourth. you can learn more here
  2. URC - The University Rover Challenge is the original rover competition. It takes place in the united states every year. Many teams from around the world compete there each year.
  3. QRC - The Québec Rover Competition takes place in Montreal, Québec every year. uORover has been invited for the first time this year and we are very excited to partipagte.
